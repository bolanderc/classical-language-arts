## Selected Work

### The Secret Garden: Before-and-After Description

At first the garden looked lonely and shut away. The earth was hard, the branches were tangled, and nothing seemed cheerful. When Mary found the key and began to care for the garden, it slowly woke up. Little green shoots came through the soil, flowers began to bloom, and the hidden place became full of life again. The restoration of the garden also helped Mary become less cross and more curious.

### The Secret Garden: Garden Plan Project

I made a garden map with a locked door, a curved path, rose bushes, and a small place where the robin could sit. The garden shows how a place can look dead but still have life hidden inside it. I used a key symbol to show Mary's curiosity.
